NAVIGATING THE HELL THAT IS DATA STRUCTURES & ALGORITHMS

Posted on October 15, 2023

In the world of programming, mastering data structures and algorithms can feel like an impossible challenge. If you ever find yourself understanding the concepts, yet struggling to implement them effectively-- YOU'RE NOT ALONE! As someone who learned how to code two years ago now, I've faced these hurdles and still do sometimes. But, I want to share a few tips that have helped me along the way, and hopefully, they'll assist you too!

1. PRACTICE, PRACTICE, PRACTICE

It's a piece of advice you've probably heard a million times, and it definitely is easier said than done, but it's true: PRACTICE MAKES PERFECT! We all have our LeetCode reminders, either literally on our phones or in the back of our minds- haunting us. However, here's the secret: it's OKAY to look at solutions. Staring at a problem for hours won't magically make you understand it. Instead, explore different solutions. Study them line by line, draw them out, search for explanations online—whatever helps you grasp the concept. Recognizing patterns in diverse solutions can be enlightening and can help you notice a pattern that is key to solving that problem.

2. READ, READ, READ

There's unlimited knowledge in crazy books that you probably never saw yourself getting into as a child.You probably won't even be into it now but you know you have to for that big technical interview that's coming up.

Books like Cracking the Coding Interview and Hacking the System Design Interview are resources that provide in-depth knowledge and real-world examples, aiding you in understanding the core concepts better.

Remember, the journey to mastering data structures and algorithms is filled with challenges, but with persistence, practice, and the right resources, you can overcome them. Happy coding!
